Rock Paper Scissors - 3

Rock Paper Scissors is a classic game that we all know ever since we were kids. This game revolves around three objects, Rock, Paper, and Scissors.

ROCK crushes scissors.

PAPER covers rock.

SCISSORS cut paper.

Rock Paper Scissors - 7

This is basically like the original Rock Paper Scissors except it now has seven objects rather than three. These objects are Rock, Paper, Scissors, Fire, Air, Water, and Sponge.

(retrieved from: https://www.umop.com/rps7.htm)

ROCK pounds out fire, and crushes scissors and sponge.

PAPER covers rock, fans out air, and floats on water.

SCISSORS cut paper and sponge, and swish through air.

FIRE burns paper and sponge, and melts scissors.

AIR erodes rock, blows out fire, and evaporates water.

WATER erodes Rock, rusts scissors, and puts out fire.

SPONGE soaks paper, uses air pockets, and absorbs water.
